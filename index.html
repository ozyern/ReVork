Will my warranty be voided? ‚ñº

Yes! Our builds pass Play Integrity (MEETS_DEVICE_INTEGRITY) out of the box. Banking apps, Netflix, and other apps requiring attestation work correctly. We use a combination of certified keys and the latest attestation bypass techniques.
Can I update over-the-air (OTA)? ‚ñº
Stable builds support incremental OTA updates from within Settings ‚Üí Software Update, just like official firmware. Beta builds require manual flashing via recovery for now, but OTA support is coming soon.
Will I lose all my data? ‚ñº
If you're coming from stock OxygenOS or a different ROM, yes ‚Äî a clean wipe is required for first installation. We strongly recommend backing up your data with ADB backup or Google One before proceeding.
