Installation Guide

Prerequisites

  • Unlocked bootloader on your device
  • Latest official OxygenOS/ColorOS version installed
  • ADB and Fastboot tools installed on your computer
  • USB cable and USB drivers for your device
  • OrangeFox Recovery Image downloaded
  • ReVork ROM ZIP file downloaded
⚠️ Important:

Flashing custom ROMs will wipe your device data. Backup all important files before proceeding. We are not responsible for any data loss.

Step 1: Enable Developer Options & USB Debugging

Enable debugging mode to communicate with your device via ADB.

  • Go to Settings → About Phone
  • Tap Build Number 7 times to unlock Developer Options
  • Go back to Settings → Developer Options
  • Enable "USB Debugging" & "OEM Unlocking"
  • Connect your device to your computer via USB

Step 2: Boot into Fastboot Mode

Restart your device into fastboot mode to prepare for recovery flashing.

  • Open command prompt/terminal on your computer
  • Reboot device to bootloader:
    adb reboot bootloader
  • Verify device is detected:
    fastboot devices
Tip: If your device is not detected, install the latest USB drivers for your device model.

Step 3: Flash OrangeFox Recovery

Flash the custom recovery that will allow you to install the ROM.

  • Download OrangeFox Recovery for your device
  • Place the recovery image in your ADB/Fastboot directory
  • Flash the recovery:
    fastboot flash recovery orangefox.img
  • Wait for the command to complete

Step 4: Boot into Recovery Mode

Start your device in recovery mode to prepare for ROM installation.

Execute in fastboot:

fastboot reboot recovery

Your device will boot into OrangeFox Recovery. Use volume buttons to navigate and power button to select.

Step 5: Format Data & Wipe Cache

Clean your device to prepare for the ROM installation.

  • In OrangeFox Recovery, select "Wipe" option
  • Select "Format Data" (this will erase all user data)
  • Confirm when prompted
  • Go back and select "Wipe Cache Partition"
Critical:

Format Data will permanently delete all files. Make sure you have backed up everything important.

Step 6: Sideload ReVork ROM

Transfer and flash the ROM file to your device.

  • In OrangeFox, select "Advanced" → "ADB Sideload"
  • Your device is now ready to receive the ROM file
  • On your computer, execute:
    adb sideload revork-rom.zip
  • The transfer will begin (this may take 5-15 minutes)
Important: Do not disconnect your device during sideload. If progress stops at 47%, this is normal.

Step 7: Reboot Your Device

Complete the installation and boot into your new ROM.

  • After sideload completes, select "Reboot System Now"
  • Your device will reboot (first boot takes 2-5 minutes)
  • Enjoy your ReVork ROM! You're all set.

Troubleshooting

Device Not Detected

Ensure USB debugging is enabled, USB drivers are installed correctly, and try a different USB port. You may need to manually install device drivers from your manufacturer's official website.

Sideload Fails

Verify your ROM ZIP file is valid and not corrupted. Re-download the ROM and try again. Ensure your device is properly connected and recovery mode is active.

Stuck on Boot Animation

This is normal on first boot. Wait at least 5 minutes before assuming failure. If truly stuck, return to recovery and ensure you wiped data correctly before reflashing.

If nothing works, contact support: https://t.me/ReVorkHub
