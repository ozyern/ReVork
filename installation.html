Installation Guide

  1. Unlock bootloader — follow device-specific steps from manufacturer.
  2. Install a custom recovery (TWRP) or the recommended recovery for your device.
  3. Make a full backup (Nandroid) and back up important data.
  4. Wipe system/data/cache as recommended by the ROM author.
  5. Flash the ROM zip and any vendor or firmware packages required.
  6. Reboot and follow first-run setup.

This guide is intentionally concise — follow device-specific instructions in each ROM's release notes.
